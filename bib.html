Microsoft

Microsoft is a Technology corporation that focuses on the the development and selling of software, consumer electronics and personal computers and services. Microsoft was founded by Bill Gates and Paul Allen on April 4, 1975, in Albuquerque, New Mexico, United States. The Microsoft Corporation is one of the largest and most influential companies in the personal computer industry. In addition to developing the de facto standard operating systems -- DOS and Windows-- Microsoft has a strong presence in almost every area of computer software, from programming tools to end-user applications.
